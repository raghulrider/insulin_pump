

Our value proposition is for type 1 Diabetic patients who find it difficult to maintain/balance blood glucose levels, and to lead a healthy personal life along with the hefty process of diabetic management throughout the life cycle.
Here, the solution is to provide patients with a continuous flow of insulin in a systematic manner lowering the number of pricks or injections in the approximate ratio of 12:1.So, the value we provide is relief from pain, at a low cost and also a sense of confidence to the patient and our products primary diffrentiation are its affordability , availability ands its user friendliness.
